Venus

  • Venus is the second planet from the Sun. It is one of the four terrestrial planets in the Solar System
  • Its orbit around the Sun takes 87.97 Earth days, the shortest of all the Sun's planets.
  • It's atmosphere is 96.5% carbon dioxide, with most of the remaining 3.5% being nitrogen. The surface pressure is 9.3 megapascals (93 bar) and the surface temperature is 737 K (464 °C; 867 °F).
  • The diameter of Venus is 12,103.6 km (7,520.8 mi) and its mass is 81.5% of Earth's.
  • Venus has a colour of Yellowish-white.
